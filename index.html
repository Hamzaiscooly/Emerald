How do I add Emerald Bot to my server?

Simply click the "Invite to Server" button on our website. You'll be redirected to Discord's authorization page where you can select your server and grant the necessary permissions. The bot will automatically join and be ready to use!

What permissions does Emerald Bot need?

Emerald requires basic permissions like reading messages, sending messages, managing messages, and kicking/banning members for moderation features. You can customize permissions during the invite process. We recommend using the recommended permissions for full functionality.

Is Emerald Bot free to use?

Yes, Emerald Bot is completely free with all core features available. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free. We believe in providing value to the Discord community.

How can I get support for Emerald Bot?

Join our Discord support server using the "Support" button on our website. Our community and support team are available 24/7 to help with any questions or issues. You can also check our documentation for common troubleshooting steps.

Can I suggest new features for Emerald?

Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions in our Discord server. We regularly review community feedback and implement popular requests in our updates. Your suggestions help shape the future of Emerald Bot!
