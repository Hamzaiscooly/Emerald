Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Emerald Bot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller Information

The data controller for Emerald Bot is based in the Netherlands. For any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our support server.

2. Information We Collect

Emerald Bot collects and processes the following categories of personal data:

  • Server Information: Server ID, name, configuration settings, and channel IDs necessary for bot functionality
  • User Information: User IDs, usernames, and discriminators for features like moderation, leveling, and welcome messages
  • Message Content: Limited message content for moderation purposes (filtered for inappropriate content) and command processing
  • Usage Data: Command usage statistics, feature interaction data, and error logs to improve our services
  • Configuration Data: Your custom settings, preferences, and bot configurations for your server

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of Contract: To provide the bot services you requested when adding Emerald to your server
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, ensure security, and prevent abuse
  • Consent: For optional features where we explicitly request your consent

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the bot's core functionality (moderation, welcome messages, etc.)
  • To improve and customize the user experience
  • To monitor for abuse and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To respond to user support requests and troubleshoot issues
  • To develop new features and improve existing ones
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our services

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular security updates
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring

While we strive to protect your personal data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Our retention periods are:

  • Server Data: Retained until the bot is removed from your server
  • User Data: Retained for the duration of your server membership where Emerald is present
  • Moderation Logs: Retained for 90 days to handle disputes and appeals
  • Analytics Data: Retained in anonymized form for up to 2 years

You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us or removing the bot from your server.

7. Data Transfers

As we are based in the Netherlands, your data is processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). We do not transfer your personal data to countries outside the EEA unless adequate protection measures are in place.

8. Third-Party Services

Emerald Bot only interacts with Discord's API to provide its services. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use subprocessors for hosting and infrastructure, all of which are GDPR-compliant.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request temporary restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to our use of your data based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for processing based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our support server. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

11. Automated Decision-Making

Emerald Bot uses automated processes for moderation features (such as filtering inappropriate content). These automated decisions are necessary for the performance of our services. You have the right to request human intervention or challenge these decisions by contacting us.

12. Data Protection Officer

We have appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) to oversee compliance with this policy. You can contact our DPO through our support server.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through our support server and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of Emerald Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Complaints

If you have concerns about our data practices, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) or your local supervisory authority.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our support server.
